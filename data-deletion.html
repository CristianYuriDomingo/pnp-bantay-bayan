Data Deletion Request

How to Request Data Deletion

If you want to delete your data from Bantay Bayan, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to: dcristianyuri@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email address in the message

We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm via email once your data has been deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your account information
  • Your email address
  • Any profile information
  • All associated data stored in our system
